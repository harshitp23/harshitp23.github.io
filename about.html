About Me

I’m a Computer Science student at the University of Wisconsin–Madison with a strong interest in backend and full-stack development. I enjoy building systems that solve practical problems, ranging from full-stack Student Grade Calculators built with Spring Boot and PostgreSQL to secure REST APIs and logic-based tools that turn complex requirements into usable applications.

Beyond larger systems like my Hotel Management interface, I regularly practice data structures and algorithms on LeetCode and build interactive games to refine my optimization and analytical thinking. I am dedicated to clean architecture and modern development practices, constantly seeking new opportunities in software engineering where I can contribute to impactful backend projects and grow as a developer.

A few quick facts:

  • Location: Madison, Wisconsin
  • Interests: Coding, drawing, basketball, and video games like FIFA and NBA2K.
  • Goals: Improve my coding skills, build accessible websites, and express myself online.
